Commission an oil painting from our local artists.

At art-portraits.net we specialize in hand painted oil portraits based on photographs you send to us. This is an ideal gift and a long lasting way to capture a special moment. We paint everything from people, to pets, to landscapes or whatever else you require.

Every portrait that we do is done on high quality canvas with Windsor oil paints and is made to last. Portraits are painted to any dimensions that you need but we can also recommend the best size according to your photograph. If you have any changes you want to make or you want to add other people to the portrait, please tell us. We can paint in different styles but our two most popular styles are realistic and impressionist. You can see some examples in our gallery.

We also write reviews of other painters and portrait artists who  commission paintings. You can read it in our Blog.
