Frequently Asked Questions

Can you change the photographs to suit the customers need?

Yes. We can change the colours or background according to your needs. We can also add other people if you send us two or more separate photographs. If you send us an old black and white photograph we can add colours to the portrait. Let us know your requirements and if possible we will do our best to accommodate you.

How do you receive payments?

We accept credit cards, debit cards and paypal. We can also accept cheques if you are not able to pay by these methods.

We ask you to pay a smallÂ deposit to begin the portrait. After the portrait is finished we will send you a photograph of the finished work and ask you to pay the remaining balance.

How long does it take to paint a portrait?

It usually takes around 4 weeks from initial payment until the portrait is finished.

What can you paint?

We mostly paint portraits of people and pets but can also paint landscapes, houses and animals . We have also had different requests in the past so just send us a photograph and your requirements and we will give you a quote.

What is the painting process?

All paintings are hand painted in the traditional way and we can guarantee you a quality piece of artwork that is second to none.

How are the portraits sent?

The portraits are rolled in a protected tube and sent to you by DHL. We take the utmost care to make sure your portraits are delivered safely. If for any reason there is a problem with the delivery or the painting is damaged we will repair it or re-paint it free of charge. We can deliver anywhere in the world free of charge.

Why should I choose you over another company?

Our artists are all experienced portrait artists and we believe in delivering a quality finished product. All our portraits are painted in the traditional way with the highest quality materials. We also let you see the finished work for your approval before we ask you to make the final payment. We also only ask for a small deposit to begin the painting which is fully refundable if you are not satisfied. We are confident you will be happy with the result.
